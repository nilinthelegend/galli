Architectural Sanctuary

Designed to Escape the Noise

Tucked inside a quiet courtyard off the streets of Lazimpat, Kathmandu, Galli Cafe & Restro is built with architectural intention. Concrete textures, wooden bar counter accents, and warm low-hanging Edison bulbs are balanced by a massive blue Buddha mural.

This is not just a room to eat; it is a physical retreat designed to make dining organic, spacious, and visually serene. We invite you to sit back, feel the ambient soundscapes, and share food crafted with passion.
